#+title: boulougouris-enhancement-2010

boulourgouris et al build on the results of [[id:e545e2db-f6de-4375-931b-3dc909793c03][boulougouris-dissociable-2008]] to show that it acts only in some regions of the brain.
They find that a 5HT2C antagonist improves reversal learning wen injected into OFC, but not mPFC or NAc.
