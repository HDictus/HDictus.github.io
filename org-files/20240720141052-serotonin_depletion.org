#+title: serotonin-depletion

It is possible to deplete an organism's supply of [[id:22bdad1e-c531-47f7-a7fe-41c2735fa34f][serotonin]] by reducing or dietary [[id:27ae4fd8-4732-4029-8bcf-38f3ac23f019][tryptophan]].
This is sometimes used as an experimental intervention.
