#+title: SSRI

Selective Serotonin reuptake inhibitor.
Drugs of this type reduce the rate at which serotonin is reabsorbed into neurons, thereby increasing the effect that the absorbed serotonin has.
Many of them also directly activate some serotonin receptors.
